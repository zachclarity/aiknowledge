📊 Gartner AI Hype Cycle

✅ Key Actions by Maturity Stage

Initial (7-14): Establish foundational AI strategies, basic governance frameworks, prioritize data management basics
Developing (15-21): Integrate AI with business goals, improve data sharing, build in-house AI expertise
Defined (22-28): Refine AI integration into all processes, create data-driven culture, strengthen comprehensive governance
Managed (29-35): Invest in cutting-edge technology, maintain strong expertise, regularly update governance frameworks
Optimizing (36-40): Lead in AI innovation, world-class practices, ensure continuous improvement and adaptation

💡 Tips for Advancing AI Maturity

  1. Start with strategy: Align AI initiatives with clear business objectives
  2. Invest in data: Quality data is the foundation of effective AI
  3. Build skills progressively: Train existing staff while recruiting specialists
  4. Implement governance early: Don't wait for problems to establish policies
  5. Measure and iterate: Track KPIs and continuously improve processes
  6. Focus on security: Integrate security into AI development from day one
